About Us

Code Yantra Community

- CodeYantra is the dedicated coding committee under the Training and Placement cell of Shree L.R. Tiwari College of Engineering. It consists of a team of passionate coders and tech enthusiasts.

- We believe in the power of community, the thrill of learning, and the satisfaction of solving problems. Code Yantra is more than a website; it's a community that encourages you to dream big, learn more, and achieve greater. By connecting with like-minded individuals, you'll expand your skills, create amazing projects, and maybe even find your next great collaborator.

- Our mission is to create a platform where aspiring and seasoned programmers alike can test their mettle and learn from each other while pushing their limits. We organise various coding related-events and competitions throughout the year which encourages the students to solve complex problems, and fosters critical thinking, algorithmic problem-solving, and programming skills.

FAQ
  • What is CodeYantra?

    CodeYantra is a website dedicated to college coding competitions and a wide range of other activities related to coding, problem-solving, and programming.

  • Are there any registration fees for participating in coding competitions?

    Most competitions on CodeYantra are free to participate in. However, some might have nominal registration fees, and these details will be provided in the competition's description.

  • Can I participate as an individual or do I need a team?

    It depends on the specific competition. Some competitions allow individual participation, while others require you to form a team. The competition details will specify the requirements.

  • How can I contact the support team if I have questions or encounter issues?

    You can reach out to the CodeYantra support team through the "Contact Us" page on the website. They will respond to your queries and help you with any issues you may face.

  • How can I stay updated on upcoming competitions and events on CodeYantra?

    You can follow their social media accounts, and regularly visit the platform to stay informed about upcoming coding competitions and activities.
